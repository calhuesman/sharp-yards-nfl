Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Sharp Yards collects, how it's used, and your choices regarding that information.

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect the email address and password you provide (your password is never stored in plain text). We also store the display name you choose, along with your in-game progress — bankroll, achievements, game history, and leaderboard statistics. This information is used solely to operate the game: to save your progress between visits and to display your chosen display name and stats to other players on leaderboards.

We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties. Your email address is never displayed publicly to other players.

Cookies and Advertising

Sharp Yards may display advertisements served through Google AdSense and/or other third-party advertising networks. These third parties may use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies to collect information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services that may be of interest to you.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.

Data Storage

Account data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party database and authentication provider. Payment information for optional cash-pack purchases is processed directly by Stripe — we never see, receive, or store your card details ourselves.

Your Choices

  • You can change your display name at any time from the home screen.
  • You can request a password reset at any time from the login screen.
  • You can delete your account entirely, which removes your saved bankroll, achievements, and history from our systems.

Children's Privacy

Sharp Yards is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Sharp Yards after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or your data can be directed through the contact information on our Contact page.
